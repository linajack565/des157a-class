Kabukicho

When you think Tokyo, you're probably imagining somewhere like Kabukicho. There's countless stores, people, and lights on this narrow road. If you're looking for a night full of endless entertainment, this is the place to be!

Pom Purin Cafe

Looking for a sweet treat? Love Sanrio characters? Stop by the Pom Pom Purin Cafe in Harajuku. You can find many cafes serving this kind of themed experience where afternoon tea becomes interactive and fun!

Cola Ceramic Art

Here is a cool vase I found in the Mori Art Museum. A nice piece of art that combines ceramics and graphic design. You can find a lot of wacky pieces in this museum!

Many Mirrors

If you're not a planner, don't worry. Simply walking around Tokyo will give you plenty of interesting surprises around the corner. One of which is this mirror tunnel! There's so many creative ways to make the outdoors more interesting.

Todai-ji Temple

You'll eventually want to leave Tokyo after being overstimulated for a few days. When that happens, check out some cool temples. I'd recommend this one as it features the biggest indoor statue of Buddha! Go big or go home.
